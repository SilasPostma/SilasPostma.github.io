About RowPredict

RowPredict is an advanced machine learning tool designed to help rowing athletes and coaches predict performance times based on various training parameters.

How it Works

Our prediction model uses Random Forest technology trained on extensive rowing data to provide accurate performance predictions. By analyzing factors such as:

  • Gender and weight class
  • Experience level
  • Training type and intensity
  • Interval specifications
  • Stroke rates (SPM)

The model can predict expected performance times, helping coaches and athletes optimize their training programs.
